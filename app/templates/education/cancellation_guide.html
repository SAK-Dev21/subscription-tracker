Subscription Cancellation Guide

Cancelling subscriptions can sometimes be deliberately difficult. Here are some tips to help you cancel unwanted subscriptions:

General Steps

  1. Log into the service's website or app
  2. Navigate to Account Settings or Billing
  3. Look for "Cancel Subscription" or "Manage Plan"
  4. Follow the cancellation flow — be prepared for retention offers
  5. Save any confirmation emails or screenshots as proof

If Direct Cancellation Is Difficult

  • Contact customer support via live chat or phone
  • Send a written cancellation request via email
  • Check if your bank can block future payments
  • In the UK, you may have rights under the Consumer Contracts Regulations

Tips

  • Cancel before your billing date to avoid being charged for another period
  • Check for any cancellation fees in the terms of service
  • Some services offer a pause option instead of full cancellation
  • Keep records of all cancellation attempts
